Bogl Explorer

* This is a prototype tool, and a work in progress...

  1. Enter a Known BoGL program in the top box.
  2. (optional) Enter a Goal BoGL program you want to know in the bottom box.
  3. Click Search to see the next programs you can learn.
  4. Next programs you can learn are listed below by name, concepts they introduce, and their content; see the following example:

    Program Name

    This program introduces the concepts: A,B,C

    ...program content that uses concepts A,B,C...
  5. Below the programs a graph shows the relationship between your Known and Goal programs. The top of the graph being your known program, and the bottom being your goal. The directed edges are followed to determine concepts and programs that show up in your search results.
